Super Easy

Slow Cooked Cheesecake



Photo by ponce_photography from Pixabay

This recipe is a delicious and really easy cheesecake. Super customizable and uses just a handful of ingredients.
By slow cooking you'll never have a cracked cheesecake again. Indulgence made easy, enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 Packages Cream Cheese, 250g each
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ½ tsp vanilla
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Ready to Use Graham Cracker Crumb Crust, 9-inch

Directions

  1. Heat your oven to 190-200°F. Oven's varry slightly so use your best judgement.
    This is also such a low temperature that you don't have to worry to much.
    You can also add a vessle of hot water, but it's not necessary.

  2. Beat the cream cheese, vanilla, and sugar in a medium bowl until blended.
    Add eggs; beat just until incorporated.

  3. Pour into your ready to use crumb crust. Store bought is fine, but feel free to
    make you're own if you have a favorite recipe.

  4. Bake for 4 hours or until the center of the custard gets to 165°F.
    It will be “jiggly” but set aside to cool. It will firm up and be ready to eat
    once it's cooled off for 2-4 hours.

  5. Top with your favorite fruit, pie filling, or fruit puree. You can also drizzle your cheesecake
    with chocolate or dust with cocoa powder. You can even add a your favorite cookies
    by crumbling them up and sprinkling them on top. Have fun and make this recipe yours!
