Fried Mozzarella Sticks



Photo by KingFisher from AllRecipes

These fried mozzarella sticks are simple and easy to make. I'm sure they'll be a hit!

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs, Beaten
  • ¼ Cup Water
  • 1 ½ Cups Italian Seasoned Bread Crumbs
  • ½ TSP Garlic Powder
  • ⅔ Cup All Purpose Flour
  • ⅓ Cup Cornstarch
  • 1 Quart Oil (for deep frying)
  • 1 16 ounce Package Mozzarella Cheese Sticks

Directions

  1. In a small bowl, mix the eggs and water.

  2. Mix the bread crumbs, half of the flour and garlic powder in
    a medium bowl. In another medium bowl, blend half
    the flour and cornstarch.

  3. Cut the cheese sticks in half this makes them easier to work with.

  4. One at a time, coat each mozzarella stick in the flour mixture, then
    the egg mixture, then in the bread crumbs. Place them on a baking
    sheet covered in wax paper as you assemble them. When you finish
    place the the tray in the freezer for about 30 minutes. This is KEY!
    This keeps the cheese from melting the second it hits the oil and
    allows the coating to get crisp first.

  5. In a large heavy saucepan, heat the oil to 365°F (185°C).

  6. Fry until golden brown, about 30 seconds. Remove from heat
    and drain on paper towels.
